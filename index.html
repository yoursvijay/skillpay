Who We Are?

SkillPay is an financial institution which provides loan for aspiring students and working professionals. We understand the current situation and wants to help students, graduates, working professionals who wants to pursue or learn technology programs. So we provide loans for the students who wants to learn technology programs via training institutes in India. Currently we tied up with 10+ institutes based out of Hyderabad and counting.

Our main objective to help aspiring students or working professionals to help them to finish the courses, and they can pay the course fee later on installment basis.
